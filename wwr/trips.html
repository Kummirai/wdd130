Desolation Canyon Adventure

Embark on a multi-day rafting expedition through the stunning Desolation Canyon. Experience challenging rapids, breathtaking scenery, and unforgettable camping under the stars. This trip is perfect for experienced rafters looking for a true wilderness experience.

  • Difficulty: Advanced

Middle Fork of the Salmon Expedition

Raft one of the most pristine and wild rivers in the United States. The Middle Fork offers a unique combination of thrilling rapids, abundant wildlife, and stunning natural beauty. This trip is ideal for families and adventurers of all skill levels.

  • Duration: 5 days
  • Difficulty: Intermediate
  • Price: $1000

Snake River Day Trip

Looking for a shorter adventure? Our Snake River day trip is perfect for those who want to experience the thrill of whitewater rafting without committing to a multi-day excursion. Enjoy exciting rapids and beautiful scenery just a short drive from town.

  • Duration: 1 day
  • Difficulty: Beginner
  • Price: $150
